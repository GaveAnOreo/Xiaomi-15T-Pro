Specifications

A flagship spec sheet without compromises

Every component is selected to support creators and power users with reliable performance, imaging, and endurance.

Memory options: 12 GB + 256 GB · 12 GB + 512 GB

RAM: LPDDR5X · Storage: UFS 4.1
*Available storage and RAM are less than the total memory due to the operating system and pre-installed software.

SoC: MediaTek Dimensity 9400+ (3nm)

  • CPU: 1× Cortex-X9 @ 3.73GHz, 3× Cortex-X4 @ 3.3GHz, 4× Cortex-A720 @ 2.4GHz
  • GPU: Immortalis-G925 MC12
  • NPU: 890 neural processing unit

Cooling: Xiaomi 3D IceLoop System maintains performance under sustained loads.

Dimensions:

  • Height: 162.7 mm
  • Width: 77.9 mm
  • Thickness: 7.96 mm
  • Weight: 210 g
    *Tested by Xiaomi Internal Labs; actual results may vary.

Display: 6.83" flat AMOLED · 2772 × 1280 · 447 ppi

  • Refresh rate up to 144Hz; touch sampling 480Hz; instantaneous 2560Hz (Game Turbo)
  • Peak brightness: 3200 nits (25% APL)
  • Corning® Gorilla® Glass 7i, AMOLED with DCI-P3 gamut, 68 billion colors
  • Supports HDR10+, Dolby Vision®, Original color PRO, Pro HDR, Wet touch
  • TÜV Rheinland Low Blue Light (HW), Flicker Free, Circadian Friendly certified

Rear cameras · Leica Summilux optics (VARIO-SUMMILUX 1:1.62-3.0/15-115 ASPH.)

  • Leica 23mm main: 50MP Light Fusion 900 sensor, 2.4μm 4-in-1 Super Pixel, f/1.62, OIS
  • Leica 115mm super telephoto: 50MP, f/3.0, OIS, up to 100× digital zoom
  • Leica 15mm ultra-wide: 12MP, f/2.2, 120° FOV
  • Master-lens system (23mm/35mm/46mm/75mm/115mm), Leica styles, filters, watermark
  • Video: 8K30, 4K120/60/30, 1080p and 720p slow motion up to 960fps, HDR10+, LUT support

Front camera · 32MP in-display (0.64μm pixels, 1.28μm 4-in-1, f/2.2, 21mm equiv.)

  • Photo: HDR, Portrait, Palm/Voice shutter, Timed burst, Screen fill light
  • Video: 4K30, 1080p60/30, 720p30, HDR10+, time-lapse, slow-motion selfie

Battery: 5500mAh (typ) Xiaomi Surge battery with intelligent power management.

  • 90W HyperCharge wired (Xiaomi 90W adapter or above recommended)
  • 50W wireless HyperCharge (wireless charger sold separately)
  • USB-C port supporting PD3.0 / PD2.0
  • *USB-C port designed for digital devices only; using non-digital accessories may cause incompatibility.

Xiaomi HyperAI suite: AI Writing, AI Speech Recognition, AI Interpreter, AI Search, AI Dynamic Wallpapers, AI Creativity Assistant.

Circle to Search with Google · Google Gemini integration.
*Availability varies by region and language. Internet connection required; check responses for accuracy.

Operating system: Xiaomi HyperOS 2.

SIM: Dual SIM (nano SIM + nano SIM / nano SIM + eSIM / eSIM + eSIM).
*eSIM availability depends on country/region and carrier.

Network support: Extensive GSM, WCDMA, LTE FDD/TDD, and 5G SA/NSA bands (n1/n2/n3/n5/n7/n8/n12/n20/n25/n26/n28/n38/n40/n41/n48/n66/n71/n75/n77/n78 and more).

Wireless: Wi‑Fi 7 capability (regional availability), Wi‑Fi 6E/6 fallback, 2x2 MIMO, Wi-Fi Direct, Bluetooth 6.0 dual connectivity.

Navigation: GPS L1+L5, Galileo E1/E5a/E5b, GLONASS G1, Beidou, QZSS L1+L5, NavIC L5, assisted GPS, electronic compass, wireless & data network positioning.

Audio: Dual speakers, 3-mic array, Dolby Atmos®, Hi-Res & Hi-Res Wireless certification.

Supports major audio (MP3, FLAC, AAC, OGG, WAV, AMR) and video (MP4, MKV, WEBM, 3GP) formats with HDR playback for HDR10 / HDR10+ / Dolby Vision® content.

Resistance: IP68 dust and water resistance.
*Tested for submersion in fresh water up to 3 m for 30 minutes under controlled conditions. Not advised for beach/pool use. Avoid charging or operating buttons when wet.

Sensors: Proximity, Ambient light, Accelerometer, Gyroscope, Electronic compass, IR blaster, Flicker sensor, X-axis linear vibration motor, Color temperature sensor.

Mobile phone, USB Type-C cable, power adapter, SIM eject tool, protective case, safety information, quick start guide, warranty card, desiccant.
