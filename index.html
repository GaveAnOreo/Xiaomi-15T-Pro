Specifications

All the numbers that matter

Dive into hardware details across performance, imaging, connectivity, and more. Availability may differ by region.

Memory options: 12 GB + 256 GB · 12 GB + 512 GB

RAM: LPDDR5X · Storage: UFS 4.1
*Available capacity is less due to system software.

SoC: MediaTek Dimensity 9400+ (3nm)

  • CPU: 1× Cortex-X9 3.73GHz · 3× Cortex-X4 3.3GHz · 4× Cortex-A720 2.4GHz
  • GPU: Immortalis-G925 MC12
  • NPU: 890 neural processing unit

Cooling: Xiaomi 3D IceLoop system for sustained performance.

Dimensions: 162.7 × 77.9 × 7.96 mm · 210 g
*Measurements may vary between configurations.

Display: 6.83" flat AMOLED · 2772 × 1280 · 447 ppi

  • Refresh up to 144Hz · touch sampling up to 2560Hz (Game Turbo)
  • 3200 nit peak brightness (25% APL)
  • Corning® Gorilla® Glass 7i · 68 billion colors · DCI-P3 gamut
  • HDR10+, Dolby Vision®, Pro HDR, Original color PRO, Wet touch
  • TÜV Rheinland Low Blue Light, Flicker Free, Circadian Friendly

Rear cameras (Leica Summilux):

  • 50MP main · Light Fusion 900 · f/1.62 · OIS

  • 12MP ultra-wide · 15mm · f/2.2 · 120° field of view
  • Leica photographic styles, master-lens presets, cinematic LUT support
  • Video: 8K30, 4K120/60/30, HDR10+, Dolby Vision, slow motion up to 960fps

Front camera: 32MP in-display · f/2.2 · 21mm equivalent · 4K30 video with HDR10+ selfie recording.

Battery: 5500mAh (typ) Xiaomi Surge battery.

  • 90W HyperCharge wired (Xiaomi 90W adapter recommended)
  • 50W wireless HyperCharge · reverse wireless charging support
  • USB-C PD3.0/PD2.0 compatibility
    *Use digital accessories for best results.

HyperAI suite: AI Writing, Interpreter, Speech Recognition, Search, Dynamic Wallpapers, Creativity Assistant.
